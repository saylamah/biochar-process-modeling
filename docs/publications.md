# Related publications and technical outputs

## Core source publication

**Enhancing Biomass Conversion: Advanced Modelling and Process Optimization for Efficient Biochar and Heat Production**  
Ahmad Saylam. Zenodo, September 2024. DOI: `10.5281/zenodo.19438451`.

Role in this repository: main basis for the Cantera reactor example, temperature/moisture/oxygen sensitivity logic, and biomass-to-biochar mass-rate interpretation.

## Carbon-management and waste-to-X companion

**Advancing Waste-to-X Technologies: Sustainable Thermochemical Pathways for Biomass Valorization and Carbon Credit Potential**  
Ahmad Saylam. Zenodo, December 2024. DOI: `10.5281/zenodo.19785243`.

Role in this repository: basis for the three thermochemical treatment cases, syngas-use interpretation, direct emission comparison, and screening-level carbon-credit discussion.

## Process-development framework

**Thermochemical Process Development: From Mechanism to Scale-Up and Industrial Adoption**  
Ahmad Saylam. Zenodo, June 2026. DOI: `10.5281/zenodo.20760609`.

Role in this repository: broader process-development framework, including feedstock-envelope qualification, product-intent design, model-assisted uncertainty reduction, experimental validation, piloting, scale-up, and industrial adoption logic.
